Game instructions:

Dragon power is a two-player game.
Player1 uses "A", "D", "W", "S"
and player2 uses "←", "→", "↑", "↓"to move
to the left, to the right, up and down.
To shoot player1 uses "Space" 
and player2 uses "Left mouse button".
You can use dragon rings to buy different dragons.
